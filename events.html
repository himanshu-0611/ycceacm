UPSURGE 2K22

UPSURGE is a National fest organized by Computer Science and Engineering department YCCE, Nagpur. It is an annual fest where you will witness technical, cultural, social, sports, literature all in one place. Pleasure informing that this year is its first year and with a view to set exceptional benchmarks, the planned events this year are SmackAthon- a hackathon, hands-on workshops, Seminars, a grand pitching event, with a spice of literature and fun of the flash mob.
